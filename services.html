Concrete Precast

Semperficon precast concrete elements are poured into reusable moulds or forms and cured in a controlled factory environment before being transported to site and lifted into position. This controlled manufacturing process results in higher quality, greater consistency, and faster on-site installation compared to traditional cast-in-place methods.

Precast concrete can also be poured on site and then lifted, offering flexibility for projects with access constraints. Our precast solutions are used for floors, walls, roofs, beams, columns, and a variety of custom architectural elements across all building types.
